Khon Vay Th Chp Vi Li Sut c iu Chnh 5/1 (5/1 Adjustable Rate Mortgage)

Vay th chp vi li sut c iu chnh (ARM) 5/1 hoc ARM 5 nm l khon vay th chp trong 5 l s nm m li sut ban u c gi nguyn. 1 th hin tn sut li sut s iu chnh sau khi thi hn 5 nm u tin kt thc. Cc khong thi gian c nh ph bin nht l 3, 5, 7 v 10 nm v 1 l khong thi gian iu chnh ph bin nht. Qu v cn c k hp ng v t cu hi nu ang cn nhc ARM.

Tm hiu thm v cch mc li sut c iu chnh thay i.


Quy tc kh nng tr n (Ability-to-repay rule)

Quy tc kh nng tr n l cch xc nh thin ch v hp l m hu ht ngi cho vay th chp cn thc hin m bo rng qu v c th tr li khon vay.

c thm (bng ting Anh)

Khon Vay Th Chp Vi Li Sut c iu Chnh (Adjustable Rate Mortgage - ARM)

Vay th chp vi li sut c iu chnh (ARM) l loi khon vay m li sut c th thay i, thng tng gim da trnli sut theo ch s. Khon thanh ton hng thng ca qu v s tng hoc gim ty vo thi hn ban u ca khon vay, li trn v li sut theo ch s. Vi ARM, li sut v khon thanh ton hng thng c th bt u thp hn so vi khon vay th chp li sut c nh, nhng c li sut v khon thanh ton hng thng u c th tng ng k.  

Tm hiu thm v cch thc hot ng ca ARM v nhng thng tin cn cn nhc.


S tr dn mi thng (Amortization)

S tr dn mi thng c ngha l tr ht khon vay bng cc khon thanh ton nh k theo thi gian, qua s tin qu v n gim dn sau mi ln thanh ton. Tuy hu ht cc khon vay mua nh u l tr n dn mi thng, nhng mt s khon vay th chp khng c tr n dn hon ton. Ngha l qu v vn cn n tin sau khi thc hin tt c cc khon thanh ton ca mnh.

Mt s khon vay mua nh cho php khon thanh ton ch chi tr s tin li n hn hoc s tin t hn s tin li n hn. Nu khon thanh ton t hn s tin li n hn mi thng, s d n th chp s tng ln thay v gim. y gi l khu hao m. Cc chng trnh cho vay khc khng tr dn ht n trong thi gian vay c th yu cu tr khon thanh ton ln nht, mt ln vo cui thi hn cho vay. Hy m bo rng qu v bit r loi khon vay mnh chn.

Tm hiu thm v qu trnh mua nh. 


S tin vn vay n (Amount financed)

Ngha l s tin qu v ang vay t ngi cho vay, tr i hu ht cc khon ph tr trc m ngi cho vay tnh ph qu v.  

c thm (bng ting Anh)

Thu nhp hng nm (Annual income)

Thu nhp hng nm l mt yu t trong n xin vay th chp v thng cp n tng thu nhp trc thu m qu v kim c trong mt nm. Thu nhp hng nm c th bao gm thu nhp t cng vic ton thi gian hoc bn thi gian, t kinh doanh, tin boa, hoa hng, lm thm gi, tin thng hoc cc ngun khc. Ngi cho vay s s dng thng tin v thu nhp hng nm v cc khon n hng thng hin ti ca qu v xc nh xem qu v c kh nng tr khon vay hay khng.

Vic ngi cho vay c da vo mt ngun thu nhp hoc s tin c th khi xem xt cho qu v vay hay khng thng s ph thuc vo vic liu qu v c tip tc nhn c thu nhp mt cch hp l khng.

Tm hiu thm v l do kh nng tr n ca qu v li quan trng i vi ngi cho vay.


Mc Li Sut Theo Phn Trm Mi Nm (Annual Percentage Rate - APR)

Mc li sut theo phn trm mi nm (APR) l thc o rng hn v chi ph vay tin so vi li sut. APR phn nh li sut, im mua gim li sut, ph mi gii cho vay v cc khon ph khc m qu v phi tr khi vay. V vy, APR thng cao hn li sut.

Tm hiu cch so snh APR


Ph thm nh (Appraisal fee)

Ph thm nh l chi ph thm nh cn nh m qu v d nh mua hoc s hu. Vic thm nh nh cung cp nh gi c lp v gi tr ca ti sn. Trong hu ht trng hp, vic la chn ngi thm nh v bt k chi ph lin quan s ty vo ngi cho vay.

Tm hiu thm v qu trnh mua nh.


Thanh ton t ng (Automatic payment)

Nh cch thanh ton t ng, qu v c th thit lp khon thanh ton th chp nh k thng qua ngn hng ca mnh. Thanh ton t ng c th l mt cch thun tin m bo qu v thanh ton ng hn.


Khon vay tr s n ln nht vo t cui cng (Balloon loan)

i vi cc khon th chp, khon vay tr s n ln nht vo t cui cng (balloon loan) c ngha l khon vay c khon thanh ton mt ln ln hn bnh thng, thng l vo cui thi hn khon vay. Khon thanh ton mt ln ny gi l khon tr gp ln nht (balloon payments) v cao hn cc khon thanh ton khc ca qu v, i khi cao hn rt nhiu. Nu khng th tr khon tr gp ln nht ny, qu v c th phi vay li, bn nh hoc i mt vi vic b tch thu ti sn th chp.

Tm hiu l do vic vay khon vay tr s n ln nht vo t cui cng c th ri ro.


Thanh ton hai tun mt ln (Bi-weekly payment)

Trong k hoch thanh ton hai tun mt ln, cng ty dch v th chp s thu mt na s tin thanh ton hng thng ca qu v sau mi 2 tun, tng cng l 26 khon thanh ton trong sut c nm (thm mt khon thanh ton hng thng b sung mi nm). Khi thc hin thanh ton b sung v p dng cc khon thanh ton cho tin gc, qu v c th sm tr ht khon vay. Trc khi chn thanh ton hai tun mt ln, ng qun xem li iu khon vay xem liu qu v c b pht tr trc nu lm nh vy hay khng. Kim tra xem nh cung cp dch v c tnh bt k khon ph no i vi gi thanh ton hai tun mt ln hay khng. Qu v c th hon thnh mc tiu tng t m khng phi tr ph bng cch tr thm khon th chp hng thng mi nm.


T khai bo k kt khon vay th chp (Closing Disclosure)

T khai bo k kt khon vay th chp l mu n di nm trang, bt buc cung cp cc chi tit cui cng v khon vay th chp m qu v chn. Trong bao gm iu khon cho vay, khon thanh ton hng thng d kin, s tin cn tr cho cc khon ph v cc chi ph khc vay th chp.

Tm hiu cch kim tra k xem tt c chi tit v khon vay c chnh xc trong t khai bo k kt khon vay th chp khng.


Tin vay xy ct (Construction loan)

Tin vay xy ct thng l khon vay ngn hn cung cp tin trang tri chi ph xy dng hoc ci to nh.

c thm (bng ting Anh)

Khon vay khng do chnh ph h tr (Conventional loan)

Khon vay khng do chnh ph h tr l bt k khon vay th chp no khng c chnh ph bo him hoc bo lnh (chng hn nh thng qua cc chng trnh cho vay ca C Quan Qun L Nh Lin Bang, B Cu Chin Binh hoc B Nng Nghip).

Tm hiu thm v cc khon vay khng do chnh ph h tr v loi khon vay khc. 


Ngi ng k hoc ngi cng vay n (Co-signer or co-borrower)

Ngi ng k hoc ngi cng vay n l ngi ng chu hon ton trch nhim hon tr khon vay th chp vi qu v. Ngi ny c ngha v phi thanh ton bt k khon thanh ton no b l v thm ch l ton b s tin vay nu qu v khng thanh ton. Mt s chng trnh vay th chp phn bit ngi ng k l ngi khng ng tn v khng c bt k quyn s hu no i vi ngi nh th chp. Khi c ngi ng k hoc ngi cng vay n vay th chp, ngi cho vay c thm s m bo rng khon vay s c hon tr. Tuy nhin, h s tn dng v ti chnh ca ngi ng k hoc ngi cng vay n ca qu v s gp ri ro nu qu v khng tr n.

Tm hiu thm v qu trnh mua nh. 


Lch s dng tn dng (Credit history)

Lch s dng tn dng l bn ghi cc ti khon tn dng ca qu v v lch s thanh ton ng hn, th hin trong bn bo co im v lch s dng tn dng ca qu v. Cng ty bo co cho ngi tiu dng (cn gi l cng ty bo co tn dng) thu thp v cp nht thng tin v h s tn dng ca qu v v cung cp thng tin cho cc doanh nghip khc. Nhng doanh nghip ny s s dng thng tin ni trn a ra quyt nh v qu v. Bn bo co im v lch s dng tn dng cha thng tin v hot ng tn dng v tnh hnh tn dng hin ti ca qu v, chng hn nh lch s thanh ton khon vay v trng thi ti khon tn dng ca qu v.

Tm hiu thm v cch kim tra lch s dng tn dng trc khi mua nh.


Bn bo co im v lch s dng tn dng (Credit report)

Bn bo co im v lch s dng tn dng l bn bo co cha thng tin v hot ng tn dng v tnh hnh tn dng hin ti ca qu v, chng hn nh lch s thanh ton khon vay v tnh trng ti khon tn dng ca qu v. Ngi cho vay s dng im tn dng ca qu v v thng tin trong bn bo co im v lch s dng tn dng ca qu v xc nh xem qu v c iu kin vay hay khng v mc li sut s cung cp cho qu v.

Tm hiu cch nhn bn sao cho bn bo co im v lch s dng tn dng ca qu v. 


im tn dng (Credit score)

im tn dng d on kh nng tr li khon vay ng hn ca qu v. Cc cng ty s dng cng thc ton hc - gi l m hnh tnh im - to im tn dng ca qu v t thng tin trong bn bo co im v lch s dng tn dng ca qu v. V c nhiu m hnh tnh im khc nhau nn qu v khng ch c mt im tn dng. im ca qu v ph thuc vo lch s dng tn dng, loi sn phm cho vay v thm ch c ngy tnh im.

Tm hiu ni qu v c th nhn im tn dng ca mnh.


T l n (Debt ratio)

T l n trn thu nhp l ton b cc khon thanh ton n hng thng chia cho tng thu nhp trc thu hng thng. Con s ny l mt cch m ngi cho vay dng o lng kh nng qu v qun l cc khon thanh ton hng thng hon tr s tin qu v d nh vay.

Tm hiu cch tnh t l n trn thu nhp. 


Chng th tch thu nh (Deed-in-lieu of foreclosure)

Chng th tch thu nh l mt tha thun, trong qu v t nguyn chuyn quyn s hu nh ca mnh cho ngi cho vay trnh qu trnh tch thu ti sn th chp. Nh chng th tch thu nh, qu v c th trnh phi chu trch nhim c nhn i vi bt k s tin no cn li trong khon th chp. Nu qu v sng mt tiu bang m mnh phi chu trch nhim v bt k khon thiu ht no (khon chnh lch gia gi tr ti sn ca qu v v s tin qu v cn n trong khon vay khi mua nh), qu v s mun yu cu ngi cho vay t b khon thiu ht . Nu ngi cho vay t b khon thiu ht, hy ghi nhn s t b ny bng vn bn v gi trong h s ca qu v. Chng th tch thu nh l mt loi gim thiu tn tht.


Qu hn (Delinquent)

Qu hn l mt thut ng khc ch vic qu v thanh ton tr hn. Khon vay s tr nn qu hn khi qu v b l thanh ton hoc khng thanh ton y trc ngy o hn. Sau khi qu v qu hn trong khong thi gian nht nh, ngi cho vay hoc cng ty dch v th chp c th bt u qu trnh tch thu ti sn th chp. Khong thi gian ny c th khc nhau, ty theo tiu bang.

Cc quy tc lin bang cng c th p dng khi bt u tch thu ti sn th chp. Tm hiu thm v cc la chn ca qu v nu qu v khng th tr khon vay th chp.


Tnh nng i n (Demand feature)

T khai bo k kt khon vay th chp c mt tuyn b vi ni dung Khon vay ca qu v c tnh nng i n, c th c chn l c hoc khng. Tnh nng i n cho php ngi cho vay yu cu tr n trc hn.

c thm (bng ting Anh)


Tin tr trc (Down payment)

Tin tr trc l s tin qu v tr ngay cho cn nh. Qu v tr ngay mt t l phn trm gi tr ca cn nh v vay phn cn li thng qua khon vay th chp. Ni chung, qu v tr trc cng nhiu tin, li sut nhn c cng thp v cng c nhiu kh nng c chp thun cho vay.

Tm hiu thm v cch xc nh tin tr trc.


Chng trnh hoc tr cp h tr tin tr trc (Down payment programs or grants)

Chng trnh hoc tr cp h tr tin tr trc thng cp n s h tr do t chc nh chnh ph hoc c quan phi li nhun cung cp cho ngi mua nh tr gip h tr trc khi mua nh. Cc khon tin ny c th cung cp di dng tr cp hon ton hoc c th yu cu hon tr, chng hn nh khi bn nh.

Tm hiu ni qu v c th nhn thng tin v chng trnh hoc tr cp h tr tin tr trc.


Tin t cc (Earnest money)

Tin t cc l khon tin k qu m ngi mua tr th hin thin ch i vi tha thun hp ng k mua nh. Tin t cc do ngi bn hoc bn th ba nh nh mi gii bt ng sn hoc cng ty chng th gi. Nu giao dch mua bn nh hon tt hoc k kt xong, tin t cc c th c dng tr cho l ph hon tt giao dch bt ng sn hoc tin tr trc. Nu hp ng b chm dt v l do chnh ng, tin t cc s c tr li cho ngi mua. Nu ngi mua khng thc hin mt cch thin ch, tin t cc c th b tch thu v tr cho ngi bn.Tm hiu thm v qu trnh mua nh.


Vn ch s hu (Equity)

Vn ch s hu l gi tr hin ti ca ti sn qu v c tr i tin th chp hin c trn ti sn ca qu v.

Tm hiu xem khon vay tin vn t cn nh ang tr gp l g.


Ti khon k qu (Escrow)

Ti khon k qu do ngi cho vay thit lp thanh ton mt s chi ph lin quan n ti sn, nh thu ti sn v bo him nh. Mt phn thanh ton hng thng ca qu v c chuyn vo ti khon ny. Nu khon vay th chp ca qu v khng c ti khon k qu, qu v s thanh ton trc tip cc chi ph lin quan n ti sn.

Tm hiu thm v cch chng th y thc gi lm bng chng cho 2 bn nh hng n khon thanh ton tin vay th chp hng thng.


Fannie Mae

Qu Th Chp Nh Quc Gia Lin Bang (Fannie Mae) mua v bo m cc khon th chp t cc t chc cho vay nhm n lc tng cng cch thc cho vay hp l. Fannie Mae khng phi l c quan lin bang. y l doanh nghip c chnh ph ti tr di s gim st ca C Quan Ti Chnh Nh Lin Bang (FHFA).

Tm hiu thm v cc khon vay khng do chnh ph h tr.


L ph vay vn ca FHA (FHA funding fee)

Cc Qun L Nh Lin Bang (FHA) yu cu l ph vay vn ca FHA v ph bo him hng thng (MIP) cho hu ht cc chng trnh dnh cho nh bit lp. Ph bo him khon vay tr trc ny i khi gi l Ph bo him khon vay tr trc (UFMIP).

Tm hiu xem khon vay th chp FHA c ph hp vi qu v hay khng.


Khon vay FHA (FHA loan)

Khon vay FHA l khon vay t ngi cho vay t nhn do C Quan Qun L Nh Lin Bang (FHA) qun l v bo him. Khon vay FHA khc vi cc khon vay khng do chnh ph h tr v cho php im tn dng thp hn v khon tr trc thp ti 3,5% tng s tin vay. S tin cho vay ti a khc nhau, ty theo qun.

Tm hiu v khon vay FHA v cc la chn khon vay mua nh khc.


Gii hn vay th chp FHA (FHA mortgage limits)

Gii hn vay th chp FHA l gii hn s tin i vi cc khon vay th chp iu kin m FHA s bo him trong chng trnh th chp nh bit lp. Cc gii hn ny da trn v tr v c th sa i hng nm.

S dng cng c ca FHA tra cu cc gii hn cho vay cp qun ca qu v . 


Ph vay n tr gp (Finance charge)

Ph vay n tr gp l tng s tin li v ph vay m qu v phi tr trong ton b thi hn ca khon vay th chp.

c thm (bng ting Anh)


Chng trnh cho vay dnh cho ngi mua nh ln u (First-time home buyers (FTHB) loan programs)

Ngi mua nh ln u (FTHB) c th s dng mt s loi chng trnh cho vay khc nhau mua cn nh u tin ca mnh. Cc khon vay FTHB ph bin bao gm chng trnh do FHA, VA, USDA, Fannie Mae v Freddie Mac cung cp vi khon tr trc thp. Mt s chng trnh xc nh FTHB l ngi cha mua nh trong vng t ba nm tr ln.

Tm hiu cc la chn cho vay ca qu v. 


Vay th chp li sut c nh (Fixed-rate mortgage)

Vay th chp li sut c nh l mt loi khon vay mua nh, trong li sut c n nh khi qu v vay tin v s khng thay i trong sut thi hn ca khon vay.

Tm hiu thm v cch hot ng ca khon vay th chp li sut c nh v nhng ni dung cn cn nhc. 


Hon tr n tm thi (Forbearance)

Hon tr n tm thi l khi cng ty dch v th chp cho php qu v tm thi thanh ton khon vay th chp ca mnh vi mc li sut thp hn hoc tm thi ngng thanh ton khon vay th chp ca qu v. Cng ty dch v th chp c th cho qu v hon tr n nu - chng hn nh gn y qu v b mt vic, b tai nn hoc b bnh hay thng tch lm tng chi ph chm sc sc khe ca qu v. Hon tr n tm thi l mt loi gim thiu tn tht.

Tm hiu thm v hon tr n tm thi khon vay th chp

Ty vo loi khon vay mnh c, qu v c th c cc la chn hon tr n tm thi khc nhau. Qu v phi lin h vi cng ty lm dch v cho vay yu cu c hon tr n tm thi. Lu rng qu v s phi b p cc khon thanh ton b thiu hoc b l ny khi thi gian hon tr n tm thi kt thc.


Bo him bt buc (Force-placed insurance)

Cng ty dch v th chp c th yu cu bo him bt buc khi qu v khng c chnh sch bo him ca ring mnh hoc nu chnh sch ca ring qu v khng p ng cc yu cu ca cng ty dch v th chp. Bo him bt buc thng ch bo v ngi cho vay, ch khng phi qu v. Cng ty dch v th chp s tnh ph bo him cho qu v. Bo him bt buc thng t hn so vi vic t qu v tm mua hp ng bo him.


Tch thu ti sn th chp (Foreclosure)

Tch thu ti sn th chp l khi ngi cho vay hoc cng ty dch v th chp ly li ti sn sau khi ngi ch nh khng thanh ton khon vay th chp. mt s tiu bang, ngi cho vay phi ra ta tch thu ti sn ca qu v (tch bin ti sn), nhng cc tiu bang khc khng yu cu c th tc ta n (tch thu ti sn th chp m khng cn c phn quyt ca ta n). Ni chung, ngi vay phi c thng bo nu ngi cho vay hoc cng ty dch v th chp bt u th tc tch thu ti sn th chp. Cc quy tc lin bang cng c th p dng khi bt u tch thu ti sn th chp.

Nu qu v lo lng v vic b tch thu ti sn th chp, hy tm hiu cch nhn tr gip.


Freddie Mac

Tp on Th Chp Cho Vay Mua Nh Lin Bang (Freddie Mac) l cng ty t nhn do Quc Hi thnh lp. Nhim v ca tp on ny l thc y tnh n nh v gi c phi chng trong th trng nh bng cch mua khon th chp t ngn hng v cc nh cho vay khc. Tp on Freddie Mac hin ang c qun l, di s ch o ca C Quan Ti Chnh Nh Lin Bang (FHFA).

Tm hiu thm v cc khon vay khng do chnh ph h tr. 


Ti Liu c Tnh Tin Tr Gp Khi Vay Th Chp (Good Faith Estimate)

Ti Liu c Tnh Tin Tr Gp Khi Vay Th Chp (GFE) l mu n m ngi cho vay phi cung cp cho qu v khi qu v np n xin th chp ngc. GFE lit k thng tin c bn v cc iu khon ca ngh cho vay th chp ngc.  

Tm hiu thi im qu v nhn c Ti Liu c Tnh Tin Tr Gp Khi Vay Th Chp. 


Ph lu gi h s ca chnh ph (Government recording charges)

Ph lu gi h s ca chnh ph l ph do cc c quan chnh quyn tiu bang v a phng tnh cho vic lu gi hp php chng th, th chp v cc ti liu lin quan n khon vay mua nh ca qu v.

c thm (bng ting Anh)

Khon vay th chp c gi cao hn (Higher-priced mortgage loan)

Ni chung, khon vay th chp c gi cao hn l khon vay c mc li sut theo phn trm mi nm (hay APR) cao hn mc chun (gi l Mc Li Sut u i Chnh Trung Bnh).

c thm (bng ting Anh)

Ph qun l ca HOA (HOA dues)

Nu quan tm n vic mua chung c, hp tc x nh hoc nh trong khu c quy hoch hoc cng ng c t chc khc dng chung cc dch v, qu v thng phi tr l ph khu chung c hoc ph qun l ca Hip Hi Ch Nh (HOA). Cc khon ph ny khc nhau ng k. L ph khu chung c hoc ph qun l ca HOA thng thanh ton ring vi khon thanh ton th chp hng thng. Nu khng tr cc khon ph ny, qu v c th phi i mt vi cc n lc thu n ca hip hi ch nh v thm ch b tch thu nh.

Tm hiu thm v qu trnh mua nh.


Thm nh nh (Home appraisal)

Thm nh l ti liu bng vn bn, cho thy kin v gi tr ca mt ti sn. Qu trnh thm nh cung cp cho qu v thng tin hu ch v ti sn. Qu trnh ny m t gi tr ti sn v c th so snh ti sn vi cc ti sn khc trong vng ln cn. Thm nh l nh gi c lp v gi tr ca ti sn.

Tm hiu thm v l do vic thm nh li quan trng. 


Hn mc tn dng cho vn ch s hu (Home equity line of credit - HELOC)

Hn mc tn dng cho vn ch s hu (HELOC) l hn mc tn dng cho php qu v vay da trn phn vn qu v c trong ti sn. Vn ch s hu l gi tr hin ti ca ti sn qu v c tr i tin th chp hin ti trn ti sn ca qu v. Khng ging nh khon vay tin vn t cn nh ang tr gp, HELOC thng c li sut c th iu chnh. i vi hu ht cc HELOC, qu v s nhn c ngn phiu c bit hoc th tn dng v c th vay tin trong thi gian nht nh k t khi m ti khon. Khong thi gian ny gi l thi hn rt tin vay. Trong thi hn ny, qu v c th vay tin v phi thc hin cc khon thanh ton ti thiu. Khi thi hn rt tin vay kt thc, qu v s khng th vay tin t hn mc tn dng ca mnh na. Sau khi thi hn ni trn kt thc, qu v c th cn thanh ton cng lc ton b s d n ca mnh hoc c th c php hon tr trong khong thi gian nht nh. Nu qu v khng th tr li HELOC, ngi cho vay c th tch thu nh ca qu v.

Nu qu v sng tiu bang m mnh phi chu trch nhim v bt k khon thiu ht no (khon chnh lch gia gi tr ti sn ca qu v v s tin qu v cn n trong khon vay mua nh), qu v s mun yu cu ngi cho vay t b khon thiu ht . Nu ngi cho vay t b khon thiu ht, hy vit ra bng vn bn v gi trong h s ca qu v.
